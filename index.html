Welcome to Nova Bubble Tea!

Nova Bubble Tea is a site where the bubble/boba tea lovers of Northern Virginia can find their next favorite shop in the area.

Simply use the map or search box and enter a city, zip code, or shop name to help fulfill your next boba craving.

Looking for something specific? Check one or more filters to narrow down your search. If you can't make a decision, click or tap the "surprise me" button, and a random shop will be chosen for you! You can also enter a city or zip code to get a surprise shop in a specific area.

This website does not take special holiday hours into consideration, so please keep that in mind.

Is there a shop or detail that I missed? Please let me know, and I will add it as soon as possible :)

Thank you for visiting!
